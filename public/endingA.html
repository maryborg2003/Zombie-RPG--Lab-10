After enduring the perilous odyssey to secure this elusive cure, you choose to hoard it, safeguarding your own future. As the world around you withers in the grip of the apocalypse, you carve out an existence of solitude, utilizing the cure as a shield against each assault of infection. Your days stretch long and empty, marked by the relentless struggle for survival, until at age 67, you breathe your last, alone amidst the ruins of civilization. The fate of humanity remains bleak, with your secret dying alongside you.
