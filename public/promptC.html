You decide to investigate the noise. The night is thick with fog, and the moon is a mere sliver in the sky, casting long shadows across the desolate street. You’re alone, save for the sound of your own breath and the distant, guttural moans of the undead. As you turn a corner, you’re suddenly face-to-face with a group of four zombies! Their eyes are hollow, their movements jerky and uncoordinated, but they have not yet seen you, and you have a chance to escape. Your heart races as you weigh your options: stand your ground and fight, using only the pocket knife in your bag, or turn and run, hoping to lose them in the maze of the city.
