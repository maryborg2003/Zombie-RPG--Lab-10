As the world succumbs to darkness under the shadow of a solar eclipse, a chilling virus sweeps across the globe, transforming humans into monstrous, unyielding beings. Amidst the chaos, you hear whispers of a potential cure hidden inside of an abandoned house in the middle of the desolate ruins of a zombie-ridden metropolis. With hope flickering like a candle in the wind, you stand at the precipice of this once-bustling city, the gateway to potential salvation or certain doom. You successfully make it in one piece through the suspiciously unlocked main entrance. The silence of the city is deafening, a stark contrast to the life it once held. You tread cautiously among the abandoned cars and scattered debris. A sudden clatter from a shadowed building pierces the quiet, sending a shiver down your spine.
