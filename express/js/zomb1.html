Windy City of the Dead

In the not-so-distant future, a solar eclipse casts an eerie shadow over the world, bringing with it a mysterious virus that turns humans into relentless, mutated creatures. As society collapses, you discover rumors of a cure hidden in the heart of an abandoned city infested with zombies. As a survivor, your choices will determine the fate of not just yourself, but all the remnants of humanity."
